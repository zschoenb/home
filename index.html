Zachary Schoenberger

I am a Metadata Analyst at University of Alberta Libraries. This role involves creating, manipulating, transforming, storing, and transmitting metadata for the university's digital assets. My work is particularly aimed at supporting the institutional repository, which is built on a Hydra stack. As part of my role, I am integrated into the IR development (agile) lifecycle, which requires continuous consultation and communication.

I get excited by all things data, especially data visualization and linked data. I am also interested in the intersection between information services and digital humanities, which is informed in part by my MLIS and MA in Digital Humanities, and in part by my work experience.

In my spare time, I love to travel (who doesn't?), to read, and to play a video game every now and then.
